# STEAM-Three-Discipline-Connections
This interactive artifact was created for a STEM scholars program at the undergraduate level. As part of the instructional and supporting materials for students enrolled in the STEM scholars program, this STEAM 3 discipline visualization illustrates to scholars the interdisciplinary possibilites of STEAM subject areas related to commitment to study and professional careers. This STEAM 3 Discipline visualization belongs to a collection showing cross disciplines of STEM 2 discipline, STEAM 2 discipline, STEAM 3 dicipline, and STEM 3 discipline.
